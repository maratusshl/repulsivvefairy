How to Care for Fresh Roses

Fresh roses can last much longer if properly cared for. Always trim the stems at an angle, remove excess leaves, and change the water every two days. Keep them in a cool spot away from direct sunlight. Maison de Ratu always selects premium roses that maintain their beauty for days to come.
